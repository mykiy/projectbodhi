project bodhi

Project Bodhi is an initiative by activists with a goal to make world-class educational resources for Schools and Colleges. Here we train students to make their syllabus more fun and educational, which is then shared under Creative Commons.

What is Creative Commons?

A Creative Commons (CC) license is one of several public licenses that enable the free distribution of an otherwise copyrighted work. With CC license one can share, use, and build upon a work. To know more visit the official site or read the Wiki.
